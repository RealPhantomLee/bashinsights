# NAICS Codes — Business Analytics & Strategic Hub Insights

**Business:** BASH Insights LLC (Business Analytics & Strategic Hub Insights)
**Purpose:** This document identifies the NAICS codes most relevant to BASH Insights' service areas for use in SAM.gov registration, contract bidding, set-aside eligibility, and capability statements.

---

## What Is a NAICS Code?

The North American Industry Classification System (NAICS) is the federal standard for classifying businesses by industry. In government contracting, your NAICS codes:

- Determine **which solicitations you can bid on** (every contract award lists a primary NAICS code)
- Establish your **small business size standard** (revenue or employee thresholds set by the SBA)
- Define your **set-aside eligibility** (8(a), HUBZone, WOSB, SDVOSB programs use NAICS to determine qualifying contracts)
- Are listed on your **SAM.gov profile** — agencies search them to find vendors

You register a **primary NAICS code** (your dominant business activity) and can list **additional NAICS codes** for your other service areas. All relevant codes should be registered in SAM.gov.

## Recommended NAICS Codes for BASH Insights

---

### 541611 — Administrative Management and General Management Consulting Services

**Plain-English Description:**
Firms that provide strategic and organizational guidance to businesses and government agencies. This includes management analysis, strategic planning, organizational restructuring, program management consulting, and general operational advice.

**Applies To:** Administrative & Operations Support, Project & Program Management

**SBA Size Standard:** $19 million average annual receipts (small business threshold)

**How to Apply:**
- This is the **most common primary NAICS code for government management consulting firms**. If BASH Insights bids on a single code for the majority of work, this is it.
- Appears on the widest variety of federal solicitations — from task orders to IDIQ vehicles like GSA Multiple Award Schedules.
- When registering in SAM.gov, list this as your primary NAICS code if administrative support and program management represent the core of your work.
- Many 8(a), HUBZone, and WOSB set-asides in management consulting use this code.

---

### 541614 — Process, Physical Distribution, and Logistics Consulting Services

**Plain-English Description:**
Consulting services focused on optimizing how organizations move people, materials, and resources — including supply chain analysis, distribution network design, logistics process improvement, and inventory management consulting.

**Applies To:** Logistics & Supply Chain

**SBA Size Standard:** $19 million average annual receipts

**How to Apply:**
- Register as a **secondary NAICS code** in SAM.gov if logistics consulting is one of your capability areas but not your primary focus.
- Relevant for DOD, DLA, GSA, and transportation agency solicitations.
- When writing proposals for logistics work, reference this code in your capability statement to demonstrate industry classification alignment with the solicitation.
- If logistics grows into a significant revenue line, consider making it a primary code on a future SAM.gov update.

---

### 541618 — Other Management Consulting Services

**Plain-English Description:**
Management consulting services not covered by 541611 — including compliance consulting, risk management advisory, change management, financial management consulting for non-financial firms, and specialized operational consulting.

**Applies To:** Compliance & Risk Management, specialized management consulting not squarely covered by 541611

**SBA Size Standard:** $19 million average annual receipts

**How to Apply:**
- Use as a **secondary NAICS code** to capture compliance and risk management work that contracting officers may classify under 541618 rather than 541611.
- Some agencies distinguish between general management consulting (541611) and specialized consulting (541618) — registering both ensures you aren't excluded from opportunities where the CO uses 541618.
- Particularly relevant for HHS, EPA, and regulatory agency solicitations that involve compliance frameworks.

---

### 541690 — Other Scientific and Technical Consulting Services

**Plain-English Description:**
Scientific and technical advisory services not classified elsewhere — including environmental consulting, safety consulting, technical program support, and specialized analysis services that blend research with advisory work.

**Applies To:** Research & Analysis (when technical/scientific in nature)

**SBA Size Standard:** $19 million average annual receipts

**How to Apply:**
- Register as a **secondary NAICS code** for research and analysis engagements that have a technical or scientific character.
- Relevant for DOE, EPA, NIH, and science-adjacent agency solicitations.
- Useful catch-all when solicitations don't fall neatly into 541611 but involve evidence-based analysis work.

---

### 541720 — Research and Development in the Social Sciences and Humanities

**Plain-English Description:**
Applied and basic research in social science disciplines — including policy research, program evaluation, public opinion research, economic analysis, and social impact studies. This covers the "research and analysis" type of work done in a government/policy context.

**Applies To:** Research & Analysis (policy evaluation, program assessment, government data analysis)

**SBA Size Standard:** $22 million average annual receipts

**How to Apply:**
- Register as a **secondary NAICS code** for program evaluation and policy research work.
- Commonly used by HHS, DOE, DOL, and social program agencies when issuing research and evaluation contracts.
- If BASH Insights pursues program evaluation contracts (evaluating whether a federal program is achieving its outcomes), this is likely the code the agency will use on that solicitation.
- Stronger than 541611 for social science research work when agencies want to distinguish pure research from management consulting.

### 541512 — Computer Systems Design Services

**Plain-English Description:**
Design and integration of computer hardware and software systems — including IT architecture design, systems integration, network design, database design, and technology modernization planning.

**Applies To:** Technology & Modernization

**SBA Size Standard:** $34 million average annual receipts (higher threshold — favorable for small business eligibility as the firm grows)

**How to Apply:**
- Register as a **secondary NAICS code** for technology and modernization engagements involving system design or IT architecture.
- Widely used across all federal agencies for IT modernization contracts — DOD, civilian agencies, and independent agencies all issue 541512 solicitations regularly.
- The higher $34M size standard means BASH Insights will qualify as small business under this code longer than under the consulting codes, which is strategically advantageous as revenue grows.
- Consider pursuing GSA IT Schedule 70 / IT Category under this code as a long-term contracting vehicle.

---

### 541519 — Other Computer Related Services

**Plain-English Description:**
Computer-related services not elsewhere classified — including data entry services, computer facility management services, computer disaster recovery services, and other IT support activities that don't fit neatly into design or programming categories.

**Applies To:** Technology & Modernization (operational IT support, data processing, systems maintenance)

**SBA Size Standard:** $34 million average annual receipts

**How to Apply:**
- Register as a **secondary NAICS code** for IT support work that is operational rather than design-focused.
- Useful for task orders involving day-to-day IT operations, data migration support, or help desk services alongside modernization work.
- Gives flexibility to respond to technology solicitations that a CO classifies as "support services" rather than "design services."

---

### 611430 — Professional and Management Development Training

**Plain-English Description:**
Organizations that provide training programs for professional and management development — including leadership training, compliance training, onboarding programs, skills workshops, executive development courses, and workforce readiness programs.

**Applies To:** Training & Workforce Development

**SBA Size Standard:** $13.5 million average annual receipts (lower threshold — be aware of this if training revenue grows)

**How to Apply:**
- Register as a **secondary NAICS code** for training and workforce development engagements.
- Used by virtually every federal agency — training is a perennial government contracting need.
- The lower $13.5M size standard means size status under this code is more restrictive. Monitor closely if training becomes a major revenue category.
- Government training contracts often appear on GSA Schedule SIN 541 51 (Professional Services) — aligning your SAM registration to this code supports future schedule registration.

---

### 561110 — Office Administrative Services

**Plain-English Description:**
Provision of a combination of office administrative support services — including reception, clerical work, mail and copy room operation, recordkeeping, and general office management support. Firms primarily help clients run their administrative offices.

**Applies To:** Administrative & Operations Support (day-to-day operational backbone work)

**SBA Size Standard:** $12.5 million average annual receipts

**How to Apply:**
- Register as a **secondary NAICS code** for administrative support task orders where the primary deliverable is operational/clerical support rather than management consulting.
- Contracting officers sometimes use 561110 vs 541611 to distinguish "doing the work" (administrative services) from "advising on the work" (management consulting). Having both registered covers both interpretations.
- Relevant for agency support contracts, administrative task orders, and program office support work.
- Note the lower $12.5M size standard — the same awareness applies here as with 611430.

---

### 541990 — All Other Professional, Scientific, and Technical Services

**Plain-English Description:**
A catch-all classification for professional, scientific, and technical services not elsewhere classified — including translation services, weather forecasting, mapmaking, photographic services, and other miscellaneous professional services.

**Applies To:** Cross-cutting — used when a solicitation does not fit neatly into any of the above codes

**SBA Size Standard:** $19 million average annual receipts

**How to Apply:**
- Register as a **secondary NAICS code** as a catch-all.
- Some contracting officers use 541990 for cross-functional or hard-to-classify professional services work. Having it registered ensures you aren't missed on those opportunities.
- Less commonly the primary code on major solicitations but useful to have in your SAM.gov profile as a defensive registration.

## Key Action Items

1. **Register in SAM.gov** at sam.gov with 541611 as your primary code and all relevant secondary codes.
2. **Write a capability statement** that references your primary NAICS code (541611) and lists your PSC (Product Service Codes) for the specific types of services you deliver.
3. **Check size standards annually** — SBA updates size thresholds periodically. Your current size under each code determines set-aside eligibility.
4. **Identify which set-aside programs apply** — if BASH Insights qualifies for 8(a), HUBZone, WOSB, or SDVOSB certification, the NAICS code on each solicitation determines whether the set-aside applies to your work.
5. **Pursue a GSA Schedule** — GSA Multiple Award Schedules (MAS) under Professional Services allow agencies to place orders directly with pre-approved vendors. Registration under 541611 is the typical on-ramp. This dramatically increases contracting opportunity volume.
